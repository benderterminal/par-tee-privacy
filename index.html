PAR-TEE — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

PAR-TEE Golf Simulator ("PAR-TEE", "we", or "the app") is a golf game for iPhone and iPad. This policy explains what data the app collects, why, and the choices you have. PAR-TEE has no account or login — you do not provide a name, email, or any personal identity to use it.

What we collect

  • Anonymous usage analytics. Via PostHog, we collect anonymous information about how the app is used — app launches, taps, which game modes and rounds you play, and settings you change — to understand and improve the app. This is tied only to a randomly generated, on-device identifier, not to your identity.
  • Diagnostics. Crash logs and performance data (e.g. launch time, hang rate, energy use) to find and fix problems.
  • Screen-recording for diagnostics. PostHog "session replay" may record anonymous, on-screen interactions to help diagnose issues. Sensitive fields are masked.
  • Public leaderboard content. If you set a personal best, the display name you type in the app plus that score are submitted to a public leaderboard. Use a nickname if you prefer not to share a real name — the name is freely chosen and is not linked to your identity.

Stored in your own iCloud (not collected by us)

Your full game history and stats (every round, your per-mode bests, activity) sync through your own iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit. This data lives in your private iCloud and the developer cannot access it; it is governed by Apple's privacy policy. Only the single name + score you submit to the public leaderboard (above) is visible to us — your broader play history is not.

What we do NOT do

  • No advertising, and no advertising identifiers (no IDFA).
  • No tracking across other apps or websites, and no sharing with data brokers.
  • We do not sell your data.

Third-party services

  • PostHog (analytics & diagnostics) — PostHog Privacy
  • Apple iCloud / CloudKit (private sync & public leaderboard) — Apple Privacy

Children's Privacy

PAR-TEE is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves; the "Last updated" date above will change accordingly.

Your choices & data deletion

PAR-TEE has no account, so usage analytics are anonymous and not tied to your identity. Your game history and stats live in your own iCloud and can be removed by deleting the app or clearing its iCloud data on your device. To request removal of a public leaderboard entry, or to revoke consent or ask any privacy question, contact us at the email below.
